About Marina Bulantceva

Music has been my life's passion for over 20 years. With a Master of Arts in Music Theory and a Bachelor's degree in Piano Teaching, I've dedicated myself to sharing the joy of music with students of all ages.

After teaching future music educators at a music college, I discovered my true calling: providing personalized piano and music theory lessons in the comfort of my home studio. For the past two decades, I've had the privilege of guiding students from their very first notes to advanced musical achievements.

My students range from school-age children taking their first steps into music to adults and seniors discovering new talents. Whether you're preparing for the Royal Conservatory of Music (RCM) exams or simply want to play for the joy of it, I am here to support your unique musical journey.

Key Qualifications

  • Master of Arts in Music Theory
  • Bachelor's Degree in Piano Teaching
  • 20+ years of private teaching experience
  • Former music college instructor
  • Specialist in RCM exam preparation (Levels 1-8)
